HashKey Chain has announced support for OKX Web3 wallet, enabling users to conveniently interact with HashKey Chain through the OKX Web3 wallet. Users who complete interactions may receive additional points rewards, providing more ecosystem benefits and participation incentives.
HashKey Chain is an innovative public blockchain dedicated to connecting Web2 and Web3, featuring low gas fees, high performance, and regulatory compliance. It provides users and developers with rich on-chain application scenarios. This partnership will further optimize user experience and expand HashKey Chain's ecosystem applications.
OKX Web3 wallet is a non-custodial, decentralized multi-chain wallet with built-in cross-chain token exchange aggregator DEX, one-stop on-chain investment tool DeFi, as well as heterogeneous multi-chain aggregated trading platform and DApp discovery features.
