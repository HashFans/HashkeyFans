# Taichu Hackerhouse Launch Event

We are excited to announce the launch of Taichu Hackerhouse, a new hub for Web3 developers and innovators in Hong Kong.

## Event Details

- **Date**: March 20, 2024
- **Time**: 14:00 - 17:00 (HKT)
- **Location**: HashKey Hackerhouse, Hong Kong

## About the Event

Join us for an afternoon of innovation and collaboration as we officially launch Taichu Hackerhouse. This event will feature:

- Opening ceremony
- Keynote speeches from industry leaders
- Networking session
- Demo showcase
